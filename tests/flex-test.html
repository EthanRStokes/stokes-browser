Test 1: Single Number (flex-grow only)

When a single unitless number is specified, it sets flex-grow. flex-shrink defaults to 1 and flex-basis to 0.

flex: 1
Item 1
flex: 2
Item 2 (2x growth)
flex: 1
Item 3

Test 2: Keyword Values

Special keywords: auto (1 1 auto), none (0 0 auto), initial (0 1 auto)

flex: auto
Auto (1 1 auto)
flex: none
None (0 0 auto)
flex: initial
Initial (0 1 auto)

Test 3: Two Numbers (grow + shrink)

Two unitless numbers specify flex-grow and flex-shrink. flex-basis defaults to 0.

flex: 2 1
Grow 2, Shrink 1
flex: 1 2
Grow 1, Shrink 2
flex: 1 1
Grow 1, Shrink 1

Test 4: Number + Length (grow + basis)

One number and one length specify flex-grow and flex-basis. flex-shrink defaults to 1.

flex: 1 100px
Grow 1, Basis 100px
flex: 2 150px
Grow 2, Basis 150px
flex: 1 auto
Grow 1, Basis auto

Test 5: Three Values (grow + shrink + basis)

Three values specify flex-grow, flex-shrink, and flex-basis explicitly.

flex: 1 1 200px
1 1 200px
flex: 2 1 100px
2 1 100px
flex: 1 2 auto
1 2 auto

Test 8: Zero flex-grow (No Growth)

Items with flex-grow: 0 will not grow beyond their flex-basis.

Test 9: Zero flex-shrink (No Shrinking)

Items with flex-shrink: 0 will not shrink below their flex-basis (may overflow).

flex: 1 0 200px
No shrink
