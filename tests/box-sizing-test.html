Box-Sizing Property Test

box-sizing: content-box (default)
Content area is 200x100px. Total width = 200 + 40 (padding) + 10 (border) = 250px
Width/height applies to content only. Padding and border are added.
box-sizing: border-box
Total box is 200x100px. Content width = 200 - 40 (padding) - 10 (border) = 150px
Width/height includes padding and border. Content area is smaller.
